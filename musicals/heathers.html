About the Musical

Heathers: The Musical is a rock musical with music, lyrics, and a book by Laurence O'Keefe and Kevin Murphy. It is based on the 1989 film of the same name written by Daniel Waters.

Characters include

Veronica Sawyer

High school senior and a former nobody

Jason Dean (J.D.)

New student, mysterious and dark

Songs include

  • "Beautiful"
  • "Candy Store"
  • "Dead Girl Walking"
